üå† Geminid Meteor Shower Peak

When: Night of December 13-14, 2025

What to Expect: The Geminids are usually one of the best meteor showers of the year! This reliable and active meteor display is consistently ranked among the year's most spectacular celestial shows.

Viewing Tips: Find a dark location away from city lights, allow your eyes to adjust for 20-30 minutes, and look toward the constellation Gemini (though meteors will appear across the entire sky).

Starry Nights Star Party

üìÖ Saturday, December 13, 2025
üïï 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
üìç Rancho Canada del Oro Open Space Preserve, Morgan Hill

Join SJAA and Santa Clara County Open Space Authority for an unforgettable night exploring the night sky!

This month's star party coincides with the peak of the Geminid meteor shower - what a perfect combination! Our knowledgeable docents will set up telescopes for close-up views of celestial objects, and you might catch some Geminid meteors streaking across the sky.

Important Details:

  • Registration Required - Limited to approximately 85 cars
  • Location is 30 minutes south of downtown San Jose with darker skies
  • Family-friendly event (best for ages 5+)
  • Dress warmly in layers - it gets cold at night!
  • Bring red-filtered flashlights to preserve night vision
  • No personal telescopes (binoculars welcome, no tripods)
  • Event may be cancelled due to rain, clouds, or smoke
